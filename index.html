CEO · Founder
Wool Hong

With over 20 years of experience in the cosmetics industry, Wool Hong has successfully led numerous brand development, product launch, and commercialization projects.

Drawing on extensive expertise in marketing, product planning, product development, and brand management, she has built a strong track record across leading beauty companies, manufacturers, and R&D organizations.

As the Founder & CEO of ONYOU Co., Ltd., she provides professional consulting and strategic guidance in cosmetics product development, brand building, manufacturing, and business growth.
